Ultimate frisbee

Ultimate, commonly known as ultimate frisbee, is a team sport played on grass or sand fields
using a disk. Points are scored by passing the disk into the opposing endzone. Any drop, throw
out of bounds or catch by the opposing team is a turnover. Additionally, while possessing the disk
a player can not move except to pivot with one foot, and unlike many other sports, the game employes
self officiating. While professional leagues have recently included an "observer" who will officiate
and rule on contested fouls, in the majority of games players call their own fouls.
